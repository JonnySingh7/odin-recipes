Juicy and Tender Steak Recipe

A perfectly-seasoned, medium-rare steak is simply a classic. Follow this recipe for the best steak of your life.

Ingredients

  • 1/2lb Rib eye steak
  • 2x Cloves garlic
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • Salt/pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Remove steak from fridge at least 20 mins before cooking
  2. Season stake with salt and pepper
  3. Heat oil in a medium frying pan at a high heat until just smoking
  4. Add steak to pan, placing away from you
  5. Sear steak on one side for 2 mins, then flip using tongs
  6. Add garlic to pan, rubbing on steak as it cooks
  7. Cook steak for 3 more mins or until it reaches medium firmness
  8. Remove steak from pan and allow to rest for 2-3 mins
