Butter Chicken

Delicious Indian Butter Chicken Recipe

A North Indian classic - beautifully tender and aromatic butter chicken. Beautiful.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs
  • 2x Cloves garlic
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • Salt/pepper to taste
  • 1 Tin tomatoes
  • 1/4 Stick butter

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a mixing bowl and mix thoroughly
  2. Leave chicken to marinate for 3 hours in fridge
  3. When ready to cook, heat oil in pan over medium heat
  4. Add marinated chicken to pan and cook through
  5. Serve over a bed of basmati rice and enjoy!
