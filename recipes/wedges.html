Sweet Potato Wedges

Crispy and Delectable Sweet Potato Wedges

Craving french fries but want something healthier? Try these awesome sweet potato wedges for that crispy kick that just hits the spot!

Ingredients

  • 3 Large sweet potatoes
  • 3 Cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp hot chilli powder
  • 2 Tbsp smoked paprika
  • Salt/pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450F
  2. Cut sweet potatoes into wedge shapes, approx. 2 inches long
  3. Line a baking tray with aluminium foil
  4. Add all ingredients to baking tray and mix with hands so that sweet potatoes are evenly coated in spices and oil
  5. Transfer baking tray to oven for approx 25 mins - adjust cooking time until desired crispness is achieved
